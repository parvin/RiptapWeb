Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Riptap respects your privacy. We aim to collect as little information as possible.

Information stored on your device

Riptap stores certain data locally on your device to support gameplay and settings. This may include your best score, selected visual style, and settings such as sound, music, vibration, and ad removal status.

Game Center

If you choose to use Apple Game Center, your scores may be submitted to Game Center leaderboards. Game Center is provided by Apple and is subject to Apple's own terms and privacy practices.

Ads

Riptap displays ads in the free version of the app. Ads may be provided by Google AdMob or other advertising partners used in the app. These third-party services may collect device or usage information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

In-App Purchases

Riptap offers an optional one-time purchase to remove ads. In-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information.

Children's Privacy

Riptap is not directed specifically to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. If you believe personal information has been provided to us in error, please contact us.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
